All of our data types that we've learned about so far have been **basic** data types. They hold a single piece of data of a particular type. Today...that all changes! In the real world, we need a way of storing **sequences** of data to be used across a variety of situations. In the pre-recorded lectures for today, you'll learn about two different types of data sequences: 1. **tuples** and 2. **lists**.
