Today are main goals are: 1. Practice writing functions; 2. Start using some more advanced graphics functions that someone else has made for us!

There are two functions that we learn about <code>oval</code> and <code>rectangle</code> which we practice using...but then also use to define our own new fun functions! This is exactly what we do in the tutorial and homework this week so try to follow along as we design each function. If you get stuck or are confused on a certain part, bring those questions to the tutorial! Feel free to watch the videos at super-speed or pause depending on your working style.
