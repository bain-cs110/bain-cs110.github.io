## Style and Content

The questions will be _more_ like the practice quizzes on Canvas than the homework exercises. This is because the homework exercises are designed to take a larger amount of time than, for example, the shorter problems we tackle in class. That being said, as you'll see in Practice Quiz 1, there will be questions that depend on you having completed the HW assignments _by your self_. If you relied heavily on other's help to complete the HWs, you should revisit them and make sure you understand them.
