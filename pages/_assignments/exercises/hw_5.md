In this homework assignment, your job is to just complete Parts 1 through 3 of [Project 1](p1). Your completed file should have an appropriately defined `make_creature` function, an appropriately defined `make_landscape_object` function (like your creature it should be made up of at least 4 basic shapes), and then you should draw to the screen at least 5 different versions of your creature (i.e. use the different inputs of your function in 5 different function calls) and at least 5 different versions of your landscape object (i.e. same thing with the other function). You can either place them randomly on the screen, or you can purposefully place them as they will be in your final project submission.

{: .blockquote-no-margin}
> **LEARNING OBJECTIVES:**
> 1. Work with functions
> 1. Work with existing code libraries
> 1. Get started on your Project.

You should use the Project 1 starter code as the basis for this project. While there's no requirement to get started on Part 4, we HIGHLY encourage you to get started on that component as well. You'll have way more fun if you do this one part at a time rather than all at once.

* * *

## What to Submit

If your functions are working correctly (make sure you use the function names and parameter names defined in the project assignment) you'll see 5 of your creature and 5 of your landscape object! Make sure to submit ONLY your `p1.py` file to Canvas by the deadline. **DO NOT SUBMIT THE UTILITIES FILE**.
